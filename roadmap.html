Development Roadmap

This is the development roadmap for upcoming releaeses of cowsay-apj. There is no guaranteed timeline associated with the release sequence, but this does represent our current plan for ongoing work. This roadmap is provided to help enterprise users of cowsay plan for deployment and system integration of future releases.

Because quality and robustness is a primary goal of cowsay, emergency bugfixes will take priority over new features.
